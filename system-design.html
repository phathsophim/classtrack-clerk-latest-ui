Chips & Badges
Two-layer model. Student status and workflow stage are always displayed as separate chips. They do not override each other. Example: a Late student with a Justified resolution shows both Late and Justified
Layer 1 — Student Status Chips
These colors are immutable — workflow outcomes never change them
Present
fa-circle-check
Late
fa-circle-exclamation
Absent
fa-circle-xmark
Absent stays red. Present stays green. Always. These are permanent status colors tied to what happened to the student, not what the clerk did about it.

Where the record is in the clerk's workflow — displayed alongside the status chip

Icon System Font Awesome 6.5 Solid
Core principle: Student status icons use circles (fa-circle-*). Workflow stage icons use time/process shapes (fa-hourglass-half, fa-clock, fa-flag). A clerk can read icon shape alone to distinguish status from workflow.
