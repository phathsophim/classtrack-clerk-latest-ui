What lives on the dashboard

1

Greeting + 4 KPI tiles

Late at sign-in · Parent replies pending · Past 24h · Signed-in-not-in-class. Each is icon + text + color (never color alone).

2

Action needed

The clerk's main work surface. All sign-ins, parent replies and follow-ups in one scrollable list with inline CTAs and quick filters. Two-clerk concurrency cues on rows being handled.

3

Today's status

Donut breakdown (Present / Late / Absent) plus per-period submission progress bars — directly visualizing §7 missing-attendance alerts.

4

Weekly attendance chart

Stacked bars Mon–Fri. Today is highlighted. Friday shown as projected (driven by future-absence declarations).

5

Attendance by grade

` with `
` headers and a percent bar per grade. Outliers flagged with a Watch chip.

6

Recent activity feed

Last 30 minutes of clerk and parent actions; deep-link to the full audit log.

How this single wireframe answers the v2 functional requirements for the Attendance Clerk role.
